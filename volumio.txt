Notes on how to set up Volumio

3) Setup from app/web

    - Mixer type - software  (http://ip/plugin/audio_interface-alsa_controller)
    - pirate audio plugin
    - spotify plugin
    - youtube cast?
    - bluetooth?? - not available on free :(
    - soundcloud?
    - mixcloud?
    - DSP

4) enable ssh at http://yourvolumioip/dev
    - username: volumio
